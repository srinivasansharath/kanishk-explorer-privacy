Kanishk Explorer Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

Kanishk Explorer does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information.

The app uses Apple Watch sensors such as speed, altitude, motion, microphone level, and location only on the device to provide exploration and activity-related information to the user.

No user accounts are required. No data is uploaded to our servers. No information is shared with third parties.
